# 20 - Real-World Case Studies

## What This Section Covers

This section contains practical case studies showing how Data Engineering solves real business problems. Case studies describe the context, pain points, data sources, architecture, tradeoffs, implementation plan, and business impact.

## Why It Matters In Data Engineering

Case studies help learners understand how technical decisions connect to messy real-world constraints. They show that Data Engineering is not only about pipelines, but also about trust, operations, communication, and measurable outcomes.

## What You Should Be Able To Do

After studying this section, you should be able to:

- Analyze a business problem from a data systems perspective.
- Identify useful data sources and quality risks.
- Propose a practical architecture.
- Explain tradeoffs between simple and advanced solutions.
- Connect data work to business outcomes.
