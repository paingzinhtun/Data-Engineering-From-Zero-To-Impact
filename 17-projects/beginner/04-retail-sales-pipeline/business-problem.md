# Business Problem

The stationery shop currently works with disconnected CSV files:

- Sales exports from the POS system
- Product catalog files
- Customer lists
- Inventory snapshots

Manual spreadsheet analysis creates common problems:

- Revenue calculations are inconsistent.
- Product and customer IDs are not always validated.
- Inventory decisions are delayed.
- Gross profit is difficult to calculate.
- Reports depend on repeated manual work.

This project creates a repeatable pipeline that loads and transforms retail data into useful analytics tables.
