# Expected Output

By the end of this project, you should have:

- SQL queries for retail sales analysis
- Results for daily sales, top products, category revenue, and gross profit
- Low-stock product query
- Customer purchase summary
- A short business interpretation of the results

## Success Criteria

- Queries use correct joins.
- Metrics are clearly named.
- Revenue and gross profit formulas are documented.
- Results answer business questions, not only technical questions.
