# Project 03: SQL Sales Analysis

## Goal

Analyze retail sales data with SQL and answer practical business questions.

## What You Will Learn

- How to query sales tables
- How to join sales to products and customers
- How to calculate revenue and gross profit
- How to identify top products and low-stock risks
- How to communicate SQL results in business language

## Business Scenario

A stationery shop wants to understand sales performance by date, product, category, and customer.
