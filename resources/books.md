# Books

Books are useful when you want durable concepts, not quick tool tutorials. Prioritize books that explain systems, modeling, reliability, and business reasoning.

## How To Use Books

- Read with a project open.
- Take notes as design principles, not isolated facts.
- Connect each chapter to a pipeline, model, or business case.
- Do not wait to finish a book before building projects.

## Practical Selection Rule

Choose books based on the problem you are trying to solve:

- Building reliable pipelines: data engineering and production books
- Modeling business metrics: dimensional modeling books
- Scaling processing: distributed systems and Spark books
- Preparing for senior roles: architecture, reliability, and governance books
