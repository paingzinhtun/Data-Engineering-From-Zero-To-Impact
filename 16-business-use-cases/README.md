# 16 - Business Use Cases

## What This Section Covers

This section covers practical business domains where Data Engineering creates value: retail, inventory, customer analytics, supplier performance, finance operations, marketing, support, and general business operations.

## Why It Matters In Data Engineering

Data Engineering should solve real problems. Business use cases help learners move beyond tool practice and understand how data systems support decisions, reduce manual work, improve reliability, and create measurable impact.

## What You Should Be Able To Do

After studying this section, you should be able to:

- Translate a business problem into data requirements.
- Identify source systems and important entities.
- Define useful metrics and checks.
- Recognize operational pain points that data systems can improve.
- Explain technical work in business language.
