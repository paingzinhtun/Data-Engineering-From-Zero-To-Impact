# 15 - AI-Ready Data Engineering

## What This Section Covers

This section covers preparing data for machine learning, retrieval systems, AI assistants, semantic search, evaluation datasets, feature preparation, metadata, lineage, privacy, and feedback loops.

## Why It Matters In Data Engineering

AI systems depend on reliable data. Poorly prepared data leads to weak search results, inaccurate models, unsafe automation, and low trust. Data Engineers play a critical role in making AI systems grounded, traceable, and useful.

## What You Should Be Able To Do

After studying this section, you should be able to:

- Prepare clean datasets for AI and analytics workflows.
- Add metadata and source references.
- Understand feature and evaluation dataset basics.
- Prepare documents for retrieval use cases.
- Explain risks around privacy, bias, quality, and stale data.
- Design feedback loops for improving AI-ready datasets.
